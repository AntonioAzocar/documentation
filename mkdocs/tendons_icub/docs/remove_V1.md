| what is broken?                                              | REMOVING OR NOT   |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------- |

| **every other joint** (all proximal joints, fingers abduction, Thumb oppose and Ring and Pinkie finger motor joint) | **MANDATORY**     |
